Join Zoom Meeting

Follow these steps:

  1. Open the Zoom app on your mobile device.
  2. Tap on "Join a Meeting."
  3. Enter the Meeting ID provided by the meeting host.
  4. If prompted, enter the meeting password.
  5. Tap "Join Meeting."
  6. Once you're in the meeting, you can mute/unmute your microphone, start/stop your video, chat with other participants, and access other meeting controls as needed.
  7. To leave the meeting, simply tap on "Leave Meeting."
